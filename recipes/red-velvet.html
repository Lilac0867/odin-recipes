Red Velvet Cake Recipe

Description

This is a Red Velvet Cake Recipe that will result in a moist, rich cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons cocoa
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease two 9-inch round pans.
  2. Make the cake: Beat 1 1/2 cups sugar and shortening together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Combine red food coloring and cocoa to make a paste; add to creamed mixture.
  3. Mix buttermilk, salt, and 1 teaspoon vanilla together in a small bowl. Add flour, alternating with buttermilk mixture, mixing just until incorporated.
  4. Mix vinegar and baking soda together; gently fold into cake batter and pour into prepared pans.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 minutes. Cool on a wire rack for 5 minutes. Run a table knife around the edges to loosen. Inver carefully onto a serving plate or cooling rack. Let cool, about 30 minutes.
