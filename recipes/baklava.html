Baklava Recipe

Description

This is a simple and easy baklava recipe that will result in a flaky, rich baklava.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chopped nuts
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 (16 ounce) package of phyllo dough
  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
